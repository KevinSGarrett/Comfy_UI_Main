# Wave 18 Pores, Blemishes, and Cellulite Pass Plan

## Pores / fine texture
- Use small or medium masks.
- Low denoise only.
- Preferred for cheeks, nose, forehead, shoulders, upper torso, thighs.

## Blemishes / freckles / imperfections
- Use small masks for isolated clusters or medium masks for a skin zone.
- Keep color variation subtle and anchored to the underlying skin tone.

## Cellulite
- Use medium masks, usually thighs or butt.
- Preserve silhouette created in Wave 17.
- Treat cellulite as a surface deformation/texture signal, not a global style change.
