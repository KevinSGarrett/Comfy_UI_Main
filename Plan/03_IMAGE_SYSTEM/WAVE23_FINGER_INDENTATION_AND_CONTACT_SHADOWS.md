# Wave 23 Finger Indentation and Contact Shadows

Finger indentation requires all of the following:

1. source hand/finger region mask,
2. target body/fabric region mask,
3. contact boundary mask,
4. pressure tier,
5. target material profile,
6. hard-anatomy exclusion masks where needed.

## Visual requirements
- subtle surface displacement at contact points,
- shadow tightening near the pressure zone,
- no duplicated fingers,
- no cut-line artifacts,
- no loss of nearby texture continuity.
