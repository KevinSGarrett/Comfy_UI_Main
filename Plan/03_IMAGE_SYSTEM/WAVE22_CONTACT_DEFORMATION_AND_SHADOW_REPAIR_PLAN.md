# Wave 22 Contact Deformation and Shadow Repair Plan

## Deformation repair types
- skin/fabric pressure contact
- soft indentation
- fabric stretch/cling
- furniture/support flattening
- prop pressure mark
- rebound/release cleanup

## Shadow repair
Contact must usually produce one or more of:
- small contact shadow
- occlusion shadow
- fabric fold shadow
- support surface shadow
- material transition shadow

## Preservation checks
Contact repair cannot damage:
- identity
- body proportions
- hands/feet/face hard anatomy
- clothing continuity
- frame composition
- environment lighting
