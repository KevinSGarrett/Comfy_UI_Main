# Wave 10 Full / Half / Close-Up Framing Rules

## Full Body

Required:

- head visible
- hair visible unless intentionally cropped
- hands visible if part of pose/contact/outfit
- feet visible unless intentionally not part of shot
- floor contact visible when scale matters
- enough side margin to prevent limb cropping

Recommended:

- 4:5, 2:3, or 9:16
- 35mm or 24mm lens look
- deep focus
- body/outfit/pose references

## Half Body

Required:

- face visible
- torso framing intentional
- hands visible if required
- no accidental crop at wrists/elbows

Recommended:

- 4:5 or 1:1
- 50mm lens look
- natural portrait DOF

## Close-Up

Required:

- identity anchors preserved
- focus target sharp
- no face warping
- skin texture natural
- crop is intentional

Recommended:

- 1:1 or 4:5
- 85mm portrait lens look
- face reference/IPAdapter where proven
