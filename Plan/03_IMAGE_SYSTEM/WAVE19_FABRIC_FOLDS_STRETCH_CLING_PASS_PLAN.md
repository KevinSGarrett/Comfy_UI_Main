# Wave 19 Fabric Folds, Stretch, and Cling Pass Plan

## Loose fabric
Use larger masks and lower tension. Preserve natural drape and gravity.

## Tight fabric
Use body-adjacent masks and tension direction. Preserve anatomy and silhouette without over-warping.

## Wet/damp cling
Use a material-state profile from Wave 18 and require contact/lighting continuity.

## Wrinkles and folds
Wrinkles must follow tension, compression, and gravity. Random wrinkle texture without contact logic is not promoted.
