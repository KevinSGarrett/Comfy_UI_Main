# Wave 26 Still-to-Keyframe Promotion Rules

A still image may become a keyframe candidate only if:
- identity QA passes
- framing/composition QA passes
- pose QA passes
- mask integrity passes
- contact/deformation state is coherent
- the still has a clearly defined motion role

## Promotion outcomes
- reject
- use as anchor_start
- use as mid-sequence contact_hold
- use as pressure_peak
- use as loop_close
