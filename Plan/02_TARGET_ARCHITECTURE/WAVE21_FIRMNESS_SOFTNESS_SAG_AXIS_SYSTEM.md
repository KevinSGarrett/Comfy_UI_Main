# Wave 21 Firmness / Softness / Sag Axis System

## Firmness
Firmness controls resistance to deformation. High firmness preserves shape and rebounds quickly. Low firmness allows deeper compression and sag.

## Softness
Softness controls how broad and smooth deformation should look. High softness creates wider falloff and smoother pressure transitions.

## Sag
Sag is gravity-aware shape response. It must respect body region, support direction, clothing/fabric state, and camera/framing.

## Profile consistency rules
- High firmness with high sag is usually inconsistent unless the material is layered.
- High compression requires contact/support ownership.
- Sag changes cannot silently alter approved body proportions.
- Hard-surface profiles cannot receive deformation passes; only shadow/occlusion repairs are allowed.
