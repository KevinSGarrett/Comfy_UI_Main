# Wave 30 Foley, Ambience, and Music System

## Foley
Foley includes small physical sounds:
- fabric movement
- footsteps
- hand contact
- grip changes
- prop movement
- furniture compression
- body movement
- hair/clothing brush

## Ambience
Ambience includes:
- room tone
- HVAC / outside air / environmental bed
- distance noise
- location-specific sound bed

## Music
Music must be timeline-aware:
- optional
- scene-phase aligned
- gain-ducked under dialogue
- never mask required dialogue or foley evidence
