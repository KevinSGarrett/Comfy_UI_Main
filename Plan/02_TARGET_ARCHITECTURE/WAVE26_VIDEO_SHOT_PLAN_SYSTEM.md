# Wave 26 Video Shot Plan System

A video shot plan defines a motion segment longer than a GIF loop and may contain one or more shots.

## Shot plan fields
- shot_id
- shot_type
- duration_target
- frame_rate_target
- camera behavior
- subject behavior
- interaction behavior
- environment continuity behavior
- continuity dependencies
- frame repair allowance
- export target

## Shot types
- static_composition_motion
- push_in
- pull_out
- orbit_light
- handheld_like_motion
- tracking_lateral
- multi-character interaction hold
- interaction escalation shot
- release/recovery shot
