# Wave 30 Audio Generation Architecture

Wave 30 makes audio a first-class lane in the hyperrealism system.

## Audio domains
- voice
- dialogue
- breathing
- body foley
- clothing/fabric foley
- prop/object foley
- environment ambience
- room tone
- music
- impacts
- action-linked SFX

## Inputs
- Wave 26 keyframe / shot timeline
- Wave 27 per-frame manifest
- Wave 28 micro-motion events
- Wave 29 long-form continuity ledger
- Wave 21 material/soft-body profile
- Wave 22 contact graph
- Wave 23 deformation/collision events
- Wave 24/25 multi-character identity/contact ownership

## Output
A layered audio plan that can be rendered, synced, QA-scored, repaired, and promoted.
