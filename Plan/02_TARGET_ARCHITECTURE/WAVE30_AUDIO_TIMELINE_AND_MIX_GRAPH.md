# Wave 30 Audio Timeline and Mix Graph

The system mixes audio as layers.

## Mix layers
1. dialogue
2. breath
3. body foley
4. clothing/fabric foley
5. prop/furniture foley
6. impacts/SFX
7. ambience/room tone
8. music

## Mix rules
- dialogue wins over music
- breath must not overpower dialogue unless intentionally featured
- foley must be audible enough to support realism but not noisy
- impacts must align to visual contact or motion events
- ambience must fill silence without drawing attention
