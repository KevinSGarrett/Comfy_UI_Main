# Wave 28 Breathing, Tremble, Gaze, and Grip Model

## Breathing
Breathing is a low-amplitude cyclic motion. It should affect torso, shoulders, neck posture, and clothing/fabric very subtly.

## Tremble
Tremble is non-cyclic high-frequency small displacement. It must stay local and cannot become full-body jitter.

## Gaze / blink
Gaze shifts should be small and timed. Eye direction, eyelids, face expression, and head orientation must stay consistent.

## Grip changes
Grip changes are tied to fingers, hands, contact zones, and pressure. They must not create extra fingers or wrong-character contact.
