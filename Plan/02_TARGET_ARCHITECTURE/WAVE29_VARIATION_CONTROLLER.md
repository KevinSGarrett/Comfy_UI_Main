# Wave 29 Variation Controller

Variation prevents long sequences from looking like copy-paste loops.

## Variation types
- pose micro-variation
- breathing variation
- gaze variation
- grip pressure variation
- hair/fabric variation
- camera/parallax variation
- contact pressure variation
- rhythm variation

## Bounds
Variation must remain inside the continuity ledger and cannot alter identity, anatomy, character count, scene role, or established state without a state transition.
