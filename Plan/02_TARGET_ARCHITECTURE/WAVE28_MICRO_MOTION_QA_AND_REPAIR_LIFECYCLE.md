# Wave 28 Micro-Motion QA and Repair Lifecycle

## QA checks
- motion is visible but bounded
- no identity drift
- no anatomy drift
- no contact ownership drift
- no frame flicker
- no loop seam pop
- no wrong-region movement

## Repair ladder
1. repair single bad frame
2. repair short span
3. reduce amplitude
4. remove micro-motion event
5. rerun shot with simpler timing
