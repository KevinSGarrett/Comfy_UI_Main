# Wave 20 Hand, Finger, Foot, Toe, and Nail Repair System

## Hands and fingers
- Validate hand count, finger count impression, thumb placement, palm orientation, knuckles, wrist continuity, and object/body contact.
- Preserve gesture and contact ownership from Wave 11 / Wave 19.

## Feet and toes
- Validate foot count, toe count impression, arch/heel/ankle continuity, floor/furniture contact, and perspective.
- Reject floating feet, melted toes, broken ankle geometry, or missing support shadow.

## Nails
- Validate nail bed placement, length consistency, polish/texture, and contact with fingers/toes.
- Nails are detail-only and must not reshape hands or feet.
