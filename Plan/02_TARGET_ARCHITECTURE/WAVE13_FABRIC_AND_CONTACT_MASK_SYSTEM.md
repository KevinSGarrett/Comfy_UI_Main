# Wave 13 — Fabric and Contact Mask System

## Fabric masks

Fabric masks cover garments, panels, folds, seams, texture patches, hems, and thin edges.

They support:

- material consistency,
- fabric texture detail,
- fold repair,
- edge cleanup,
- outfit lock protection,
- contact/tension boundaries.

## Contact masks

Contact masks cover where two visual elements meet.

Examples:

- hand/object contact,
- person/prop contact,
- foot/floor contact,
- person/person boundary,
- fabric/body contact,
- hair/face contact,
- object/surface contact.

## Contact evidence

A contact mask is not enough by itself. Runtime evidence must also show plausible occlusion, grounding, contact shadow, and no mask bleed.
