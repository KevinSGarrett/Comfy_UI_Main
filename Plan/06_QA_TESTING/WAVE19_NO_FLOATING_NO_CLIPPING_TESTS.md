# Wave 19 No-Floating / No-Clipping Tests

## Floating failures
- visible gap between body and chair/bed/floor
- prop suspended without hand/surface support
- clothing detached from body contour

## Clipping failures
- prop cuts through fingers/body
- clothing merges into skin
- limbs vanish into furniture
- support surface penetrates body silhouette

## Required response
The orchestrator must rerun, switch pass strategy, or block promotion.
