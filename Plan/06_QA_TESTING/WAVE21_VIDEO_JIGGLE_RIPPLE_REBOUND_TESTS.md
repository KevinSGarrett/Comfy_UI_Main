# Wave 21 Video Jiggle / Ripple / Rebound Tests

Video motion behavior must be tested over frame windows.

## Required tests
- stable mask tracking
- stable material profile id
- rebound timing
- damping consistency
- no unexplained frame-to-frame deformation jumps
- no pose or identity drift

A single still image cannot prove video jiggle, bounce, ripple, or rebound.
