# Wave 28A Micro-Motion Decision QA Gates

A micro-motion decision fails if:
- the motion is not connected to a scene state,
- breathing intensity contradicts exertion/fatigue state,
- gaze direction jumps without a timeline reason,
- grip changes without contact/prop support,
- bounce/ripple occurs without a force source,
- hair/fabric motion ignores head/body/environment movement,
- micro-motion causes identity drift, anatomy drift, flicker, or wrong-character motion.
