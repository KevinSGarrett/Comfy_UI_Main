# Wave 31 Pose-Audio QA Gates

A pose-linked audio event cannot promote unless:

- audio force matches visual force
- timing matches visual frame/time
- source ownership is correct
- material sound matches contact/object material
- distance/gain are plausible
- pan follows camera-relative position
- reverb follows room/environment
- occlusion/muffling follows obstruction/off-camera state
- no clipping or missing required audio
