# Wave 20 Video Hard-Anatomy Temporal Continuity Interface

Video/GIF support is in scope. Hard-anatomy repair must become temporally stable across frames.

## Required temporal checks
- face identity consistency across frames
- hand/finger count stability
- foot/toe contact stability
- eye gaze continuity
- mouth/teeth flicker prevention
- nail/detail flicker prevention

## Rule
A frame-level repair cannot introduce flicker or region drift in adjacent frames.
