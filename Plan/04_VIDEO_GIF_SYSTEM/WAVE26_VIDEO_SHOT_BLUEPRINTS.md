# Wave 26 Video Shot Blueprints

## Shot blueprint examples

### A. static interaction shot
- use when motion is subtle and continuity is more important than camera movement

### B. push-in emphasis shot
- use when the motion emphasis is local contact, face reaction, or surface detail change

### C. full-body choreography shot
- use when the movement depends on whole-body blocking and spatial relation between characters

### D. sequence of micro-shots
- use when one long shot would create too much temporal drift or correction burden
