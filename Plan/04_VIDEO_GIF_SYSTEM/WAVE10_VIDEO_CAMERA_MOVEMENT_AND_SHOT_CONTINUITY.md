# Wave 10 Video Camera Movement and Shot Continuity

## Goal

Prepare the image camera plan to become video camera control.

## Video Camera Fields

Future video plans should include:

- starting shot size
- ending shot size
- lens profile
- camera motion type
- motion intensity
- stabilization
- subject tracking target
- focus target
- frame-to-frame continuity goals

## Motion Types

- locked tripod
- handheld subtle
- pan
- tilt
- dolly
- zoom
- orbit
- rack focus
- follow subject

## QA

Video camera QA must check:

- no sudden unplanned zoom
- no identity drift during camera motion
- no body scale pop
- no scene scale mismatch
- no focus hunting unless intended
- no environment warping
