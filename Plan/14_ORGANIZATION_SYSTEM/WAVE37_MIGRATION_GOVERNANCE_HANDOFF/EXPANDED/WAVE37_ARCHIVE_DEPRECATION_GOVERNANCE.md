# Wave 37 Archive and Deprecation Governance

## Archive states

- active
- candidate
- superseded
- deprecated
- rejected
- quarantine
- safe_to_delete_later

## Required archive metadata

- artifact_id
- previous_path
- archive_path
- reason
- replaced_by
- archived_at
- safe_to_delete
- owner_domain

## Rule

Do not leave old files ambiguous. Mark them active, superseded, archived, or quarantine.
