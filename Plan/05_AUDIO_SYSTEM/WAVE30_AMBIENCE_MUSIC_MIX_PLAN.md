# Wave 30 Ambience, Music, and Mix Plan

## Ambience
Ambience should be chosen from environment state and room size/material.

## Music
Music is optional and must be declared. It should be scene-phase aware and duck under dialogue.

## Mix QA
- no clipping
- dialogue intelligible
- ambience not too loud
- foley not too quiet or too noisy
- music does not mask important events
