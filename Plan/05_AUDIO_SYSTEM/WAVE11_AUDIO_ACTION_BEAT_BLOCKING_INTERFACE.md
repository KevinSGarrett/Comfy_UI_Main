# Wave 11 Audio Action Beat Blocking Interface

## Purpose

Audio is not controlled by pose maps, but action timing and shot blocking affect audio timing.

## Audio Binding Examples

- footsteps align with walking pose sequence;
- object handling sounds align with hand/prop actions;
- room acoustics match environment/depth plan;
- dialogue mouth/face motion later binds to face-control and video timing.

## Boundary

Wave 11 defines the action-beat interface only. Full audio generation and AV sync remain separate audio/video waves.
