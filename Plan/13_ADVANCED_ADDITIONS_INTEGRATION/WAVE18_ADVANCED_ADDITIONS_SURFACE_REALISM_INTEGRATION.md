# Wave 18 Advanced Additions Integration

The advanced additions are integrated at Wave 18 primarily through:
- surface-state taxonomy expansion
- pressure-mark and contact-state realism
- fabric/skin shared continuity rules
- App Mode controls for regional realism
- QA gates for skin, fabric, and contact-driven surface response

Any future advanced additions that affect microdetail or material response must register a new surface profile instead of bypassing the Wave 18 contract system.
