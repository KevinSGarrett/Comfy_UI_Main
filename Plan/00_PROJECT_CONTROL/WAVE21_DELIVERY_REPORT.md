# Wave 21 Delivery Report

Wave 21 adds a first-class **Soft-Body Material Profiles** layer to the cumulative blueprint.

## Delivered
- Material profile architecture for firmness, softness, sag, bounce, ripple, jiggle, compression, and rebound.
- Body/fabric/furniture region-binding rules.
- Still-image and video continuity interfaces.
- QA gates for profile match, deformation plausibility, contact/support ownership, and preservation.
- Registries for material profiles, motion profiles, compression/rebound profiles, pass profiles, rerun policy, and App Mode controls.
- Schemas and examples for contracts and evidence reports.
- Local validation and scoring scripts.

## Promotion boundary
Still-image soft-body passes require masks, evidence, and QA. Video motion behavior requires temporal frame evidence before promotion.
